About Miniforest Agro Farms

Founded in 2008 by a businessman Mohammed Sazzad Kamal Chowdhury, Miniforest began as a tea plantation and evolved into a multi‑sector regenerative enterprise. Our philosophy: “soil first, profit follows.” With zero‑burn farming, rain water harvesting, and community education, we are a model for tropical agro‑ecology.

Mission & Vision

Sustainably increase agro‑exports while expanding eco‑tourism, reaching 150 acres by 2030, and remain carbon positive.

Leadership

Mohammed Sazzad Kamal Chowdhury, Founder – 25 years in agriculture & business.

Tea Plantation

Area & Terroir

4 acres dedicated to white tea, green tea and specialty black tea cultivation.

Processing

Withering, rolling and oxidation in our hand-operated processing facility.

Wholesale Supply

6,000 kg/year supplied to packers and specialty blenders.

Rubber Plantation

Hevea Brasiliensis

9 acres under clone RRII 105. Daily tapping with eco-friendly coagulants.

Production

80 MT/year of RSS grade sheets supplied to the automotive sector.

Industry Partners

Long-term contracts with national manufacturers and exporters.

Fruits Garden

Mango Varieties

Rupali, Barma and premium seasonal varieties (Harvest: April–June).

Diversified Crops

Mango, orange, star fruit, guava and jackfruit with integrated pest management.

Supply Chain

Direct distribution to premium retailers and processing units.

Lake Aquaculture

1 Acre Lake

Polyculture of catla, rohu, common carp and tilapia.

Water Quality

Solar aerators and antibiotic-free management practices.

Annual Harvest

40 kg/year of responsibly farmed freshwater fish.
